Lasagne

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish featuring wide, flat pasta sheets layered with rich fillings like meat ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, and cheeses (ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan), then baked until bubbly and golden, representing comfort food with countless regional variations. It can refer to the broad pasta noodle itself or the assembled casserole.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk italian sausage
  • 4 (15 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 (16 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • Half teaspoon dried thyme
  • quarter teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • third of a cup of parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
