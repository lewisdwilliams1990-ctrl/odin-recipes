Lasagne

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish featuring wide, flat pasta sheets layered with rich fillings like meat ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, and cheeses (ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan), then baked until bubbly and golden, representing comfort food with countless regional variations. It can refer to the broad pasta noodle itself or the assembled casserole.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk italian sausage
  • 4 (15 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 (16 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • Half teaspoon dried thyme
  • quarter teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • third of a cup of parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cook ground beef and sausage in a large pot over medium heat;drain. Stir in tomato sauca, mushrooms, garlic salt, oregano, thyme and basil; season with salt and black pepper then simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles with olive oil in the boiling water, stirring occasionaly, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain.
  4. Meanwhile combine ricotta cheese, eggs and Parmesan cheese in a bowl
  5. Cover bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish with a thin layer of meat sauca; layer 3 lasagna noodles over the top. Spread about 1/3 ricotta cheese mixture over noodles; sprinkle about 1/4 mozzarella cheese over the top. Spoon about 1/3 meat sauca over mozzarella cheese. Repeat layering twice more, finishing with remaining mozarella cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese melts and sauce bubbles, about 90minutes. Cover lasagna wioth foil if cheese begins to get too dark. Let it sit before serving for 10 to 15minutes.
